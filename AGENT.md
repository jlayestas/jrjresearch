# JRJ Research Website Context

## Project Overview

This folder is for a new website for JRJ Research, a market research company focused on participant recruitment and field logistics.

The company should be positioned as a LATAM-focused recruitment and logistics partner with priority coverage in Mexico and Colombia. United States recruitment is available, but it is a secondary offering rather than the core positioning.

## Business Context

- Business type: Market research company specializing in participant recruitment and logistics.
- Primary markets: Latin America, especially Mexico and Colombia.
- Secondary market: United States.
- Experience: 20+ years in the market research industry.
- Industry credibility: ESOMAR member.
- Technology: Proprietary internal CRM for participant management, screening, validation, and recruitment operations.

## Core Services

- Participant recruitment for qualitative and quantitative studies.
- Participant profiling, screening, and validation.
- Database management through the company's internal CRM.
- Recruitment for in-person and online research.
- Full logistics support for in-person and remote studies.
- Facility coordination, venue management, moderator coordination, incentive handling, participant payments, quality control, and no-show management.

## Study Methodologies

- Focus groups, both in-person and online.
- One-on-one in-depth interviews.
- Expert interviews.
- Ethnographies, including home visits and workplace observation.
- Usability testing on site.
- Usability testing in home.

## Industries Served

JRJ Research serves a broad set of industries, including automotive, food and beverage, artificial intelligence, technology and software, app development, beauty and personal care, healthcare and pharmaceuticals, finance and banking, retail and consumer goods, and telecommunications.

## Website Goals

- Present JRJ Research as a leading LATAM-focused recruitment and logistics partner.
- Generate qualified leads from market research companies, brands, and agencies.
- Clearly explain services, methodologies, industries, and geographic coverage.
- Build credibility through the company's 20+ years of experience, ESOMAR membership, and proprietary CRM.
- Make it easy for prospective clients to request a quote or contact the team.

## Recommended Site Structure

- Home: Clear value proposition, key stats, ESOMAR credibility, and primary call to action.
- About Us: Company story, experience, ESOMAR membership, team, and CRM capabilities.
- Services: Detailed breakdown of recruitment, study methodologies, and logistics support.
- Industries: Sectors served with short, practical descriptions.
- Geographic Coverage: LATAM focus with Mexico and Colombia emphasized, plus US availability.
- Contact / Request a Quote: Form fields for project type, country, timeline, methodology, and contact details.

Optional supporting pages may include case studies, resources or blog posts, FAQ, privacy policy, and terms.

## Design Direction

The site should feel professional, trustworthy, warm, and expert. Avoid a cold corporate tone.

Use a clean, responsive, mobile-first design. The brief recommends deep navy or blue tones with a warm accent, while keeping imagery culturally sensitive to Mexico and Colombia. The ESOMAR badge or membership reference should be visible in prominent areas such as the header, footer, or credibility sections.

The website should support bilingual content, with Spanish as the primary language and English as the secondary language.

## Technical Expectations

- Contact or quote request form with email notification.
- CMS-friendly structure so non-technical staff can update content.
- Bilingual Spanish / English support.
- Client logo section that can be updated easily.
- SEO metadata, schema markup, and sitemap.
- Analytics support, such as Google Analytics or equivalent.
- Cookie consent for GDPR and LATAM compliance needs.
- HTTPS-ready deployment.
- Potential integrations: HubSpot or similar CRM, LinkedIn Pixel, Meta Pixel, WhatsApp Business button, Google Maps embed, and optional live chat.

## Messaging Notes

Core messaging should reinforce:

- 20+ years of experience in qualitative research recruitment across Latin America.
- ESOMAR-certified partner with global standards and local expertise.
- Full-service logistics for in-person and online studies in Mexico and Colombia.
- Proprietary CRM technology for faster and more accurate participant recruitment.
- Trusted partner for market research firms, brands, and agencies.

Keep copy professional, clear, and partner-focused.
